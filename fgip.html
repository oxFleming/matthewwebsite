The Vision

A Community
Built to Last

FGIP Legacy Luxury Estate was conceived to create a secure, modern, and fully integrated community where families can live, work, learn, invest, and thrive. The project combines premium residential offerings, community infrastructure, lifestyle amenities, and long-term value creation within one carefully planned development.

Inspired by the standards of world-class residential communities across North America, Europe, and Asia, the estate seeks to establish a new benchmark for quality living within one of West Africa's fastest-growing economic regions — delivering lasting value for residents, stakeholders, and investors.
