Meet Matthew Kalesanwo

Matthew Kalesanwo is a revenue growth, strategic partnerships, and capital development executive with more than 20 years of experience helping organizations drive revenue expansion, improve operational performance, build strategic alliances, and execute transformational growth initiatives across technology, SaaS, cybersecurity, healthcare, workforce management, consulting, manufacturing, automation, aviation, oil and gas, commercial real estate, and large-scale development.

Known for combining strategic thinking with disciplined execution, Matthew has built a reputation for identifying growth opportunities, developing high-value relationships, and helping organizations navigate complex business challenges while maintaining a focus on measurable outcomes.

His experience spans leadership roles across the United States, the United Kingdom, and West Africa — providing a global perspective on business development, market expansion, stakeholder engagement, and organizational growth.

A Career Built on Growth

Matthew's professional journey has been defined by a commitment to helping organizations grow. From enterprise sales and business development to strategic partnerships and operational transformation, he has worked with executive teams, business owners, investors, and public- and private-sector stakeholders to identify opportunities, solve challenges, and build scalable growth strategies.

His leadership style is rooted in accountability, collaboration, transparency, and execution — built on the belief that organizations create value by aligning people, processes, strategy, and resources around clearly defined objectives.

Enterprise Sales & Business Development

Built a strong foundation in enterprise sales, pipeline development, and business development — working with organizations across technology, SaaS, healthcare, and professional services to grow revenue and expand market presence.

Strategic Partnerships & Market Expansion

Expanded into strategic partnerships, channel development, and go-to-market leadership — helping organizations access new markets, build alliances, and create sustainable growth through relationship-driven strategies.

Cross-Industry Leadership & Advisory

Applied expertise across manufacturing, automation, aviation, oil and gas, cybersecurity, and workforce management — developing a multi-industry perspective that informs strategic guidance and executive advisory work.

Capital Development & Real Estate

Moved into capital development and real estate, advancing the FGIP Legacy Luxury Estate initiative — a transformational project representing one of West Africa's significant residential development opportunities.

Executive Leadership, Advisory & Growth

Currently focused on executive leadership opportunities, strategic advisory engagements, and high-value partnership development across U.S., U.K., and West African markets.
